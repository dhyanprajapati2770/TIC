Rainbow veggie bowl

A vibrant, nutrient-packed bowl filled with colorful vegetables, wholesome grains, and a creamy tahini dressing. This Rainbow Veggie Bowl is not only visually stunning but also loaded with fresh flavors and textures. Perfect for a healthy lunch or light dinner, it's vegan-friendly and easily customizable with your favorite seasonal veggies.

(4.0)
10 mins
1 servings

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa or brown rice
  • 1 medium sweet potato, diced and roaste
  • 1 cup purple cabbage, thinly sliced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup corn kernels fresh or roasted
  • 1/4 cup radishes, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup microgreens or sprouts (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook quinoa or brown rice according to package instructions. Fluff with a fork and set aside.
  2. Roast the Sweet Potato
  3. . Chop & Prep Veggies
  4. Make the Tahini Dressing
  5. Arrange Veggies: Place sweet potatoes, cabbage, tomatoes, avocado, cucumber, carrots, corn, and radishes in sections for a rainbow effect.
